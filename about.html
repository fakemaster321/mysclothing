About us

MYS CLOTHING

Mark Your Style is a Clothing Company, where your style speaks louder than words! MYS is about expressing your own personality via fashion, not simply clothes. Our collections combine stylish street vibes with a touch of your personality, providing comfortable, long-lasting, and eco-friendly products.

Our Story

Born out of a passion for fashion and a desire to celebrate uniqueness, MARK YOUR STYLE is more than just a brand—it's a movement. We understand that clothing is not just about covering your body; it's a powerful form of self-expression, a canvas to showcase your personality and aspirations.

Founded in 2019 and headquartered in the vibrant Barangay Camaman-an, Cagayan de Oro City, we set out on a mission to redefine the fashion landscape by offering a collection that transcends trends and embraces the diverse styles that make you, well, you. Our journey began with a commitment to quality craftsmanship, attention to detail, and a dedication to making every piece a reflection of your identity..

Our Philosophy

At MARK YOUR STYLE, we believe that style is not just about following the latest trends but about creating a narrative that is uniquely yours. Our philosophy revolves around empowering individuals to embrace their authenticity, embrace their stories, and let their style speak volumes.

We strive to provide a curated collection that goes beyond fashion, offering timeless pieces that effortlessly blend comfort, quality, and trend-setting design. Each garment is crafted with care, ensuring that when you wear MARK YOUR STYLE, you wear a piece of art that tells your story.

Our Commitment

Quality is at the heart of everything we do. We source the finest materials, work with skilled artisans, and employ ethical practices to ensure that every garment is a testament to our commitment to excellence. From the drawing board to the final stitch, we take pride in delivering clothing that not only looks good but feels good too.

MARK YOUR STYLE is more than a label; it's a promise of individuality, quality, and style. We invite you to explore our collection, discover pieces that resonate with your personality, and join us in celebrating the art of self-expression through fashion.

Thank you for choosing MARK YOUR STYLE—a brand that understands and celebrates the unique masterpiece that is you.
